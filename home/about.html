ABOUT ΣΧ Lambda Mu

CHAPTER HISTORY

During the Fall semester of 2008, 14 men came together in the hope of establishing a Sigma Chi chapter that will prove to instill the values of friendship, justice, and learning among every member. Currently the Lambda Mu chapter of Sigma Chi is the newest chapter on VSU's campus and are already one of the top chapters on campus. With diligence and a strong work ethic, the brothers of Sigma Chi have proven themselves thus far in creating a great legacy for the future. Our chapter has recieved awards including, highest overall GPA among IFC fraternities on campus, the highest new member GPA among IFC fraternities on campus, and a chapter excellence award for academic achievement just to name a few. Sigma Chi is a fraternity devoted to lifelong dedication to the morals in which it instills in its members along with a chance for an everlasting brotherhood. Along with the great experience Sigma Chi offers you while in college, the Sigma Chi lifestyle plays a major role in our life every day. Any Sigma Chi brother you come in contact with will never say they "were" a Sigma Chi but that they "are" a Sigma Chi. There are seven main elements which Sigma Chi brothers and pledges must posses in order to be admitted to membership in Sigma Chi. These are stated in the Jordan Standard and consist of...

A Man of Good Character.......

A Student of Fair Ability.......

With Ambitious Purposes.......

A Congenial Disposition.......

Possessed of Good Morals.......

Having a High Sense of Honor and.......

A Deep Sense of Personal Responsibility.
